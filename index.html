Key Insights

Manufacturer Safety Trends

Boeing has consistently experienced the highest number of incidents throughout 1995-2016, with a notable spike around 2010-2011. McDonnell Douglas shows a declining trend since 1997.

Flight Phase Risk

Taxi, Takeoff, and Landing phases account for the highest number of incidents overall. Taxi incidents have a high proportion of Non-Fatal outcomes, while Takeoff incidents show higher Incident-level severity.

Severity by Phase

While Cruise has fewer total incidents than Taxi, Takeoff, or Landing, it shows a higher proportion of Fatal incidents. Approach and Climb phases have similar severity distributions.
